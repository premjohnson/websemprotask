This is an H1 Heading

The largest heading, used for main titles or the most important information.

This is an H2 Heading

Used for subheadings or sections under the main title. Smaller than H1 but still prominent.

This is an H3 Heading

Commonly used for sub-subheadings, or sections under an H2 heading.

This is an H4 Heading

Smaller than H3, used for less important section titles.

This is an H5 Heading

Used for very specific subsections, typically small in size.

This is an H6 Heading

The smallest heading, often used for minor details or very specific sections.
